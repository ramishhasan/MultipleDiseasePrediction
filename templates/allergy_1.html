ALLERGY MEDICATION INFORMATION



AGE (Infants and toddlers)

Age group of under 2 Years


What To Do

Antihistamines: There are some antihistamines that can be used for children under 2, but they should only be given under a doctor's supervision.

Nasal saline drops or sprays: These can help clear out mucus and allergens from the nose.



It's important to note that you should always talk to a doctor or pharmacist before starting any new medication, especially if you have any underlying health conditions or are taking other medications.





POINTS TO REMEMBER


It's important to note that you should always talk to a doctor or pharmacist before starting any new medication, especially if you have any underlying health conditions or are taking other medications.

There are many different types of allergy medications available, and the appropriate medication and dosage will depend on the individual's age and specific allergy symptoms.
